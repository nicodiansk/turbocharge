# FLUX.1-Krea-dev Prompt — Turbocharge Logo & Banner

## Logo prompt (1:1, 1024×1024)

A minimalist logo of an abstract vertical spine composed of ten distinct rectangular segments, each segment slightly different but precisely aligned along a single axis. Two-tone palette: deep ink-blue (#0B1E3F) on warm off-white (#F5F1E8). Clean geometric forms, faintly tactile paper texture, soft natural lighting from upper-left, no shadows. Honest, structural, restrained. Centered composition on a square canvas, generous negative space.

## Banner prompt (16:9, 1920×1080)

Same as logo prompt, append: `, wide horizontal composition, spine centered with breathing room left and right, banner aspect ratio.`

## Negative prompt (use for both)

glossy, chrome, metallic, futuristic, swirling energy, neural network, glowing, holographic, rainbow gradient, lens flare, 3D render, cinematic, dramatic lighting, sci-fi, cyberpunk, text, letters, words, watermark, signature

## Iteration parameters

- Steps: 28
- Guidance: 4.5
- Logo aspect: 1:1 → 1024×1024
- Banner aspect: 16:9 → 1920×1080
- Try seeds: 1, 17, 42 (pick the cleanest segment alignment with least AI-texture artifacts)

## What good looks like

- Ten clearly distinguishable segments stacked vertically, all aligned to a single center axis
- Subtle variation in segment width/height (not identical bricks)
- Two colors only — no rainbow, no gradient overlays
- Looks like it could be embossed on paper, not rendered in 3D
- No text anywhere — pure visual mark
